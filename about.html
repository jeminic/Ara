Home / About Our mission

Strategic answers to a crisis that keeps repeating itself.

African Refugees Aid develops evidence-led approaches to the root causes of the direct and structural violence that produce refugees and internally displaced persons — then does the practical, on-the-ground work of Facilitating Development in Nigeria and across Africa.

—

Why we exist

Millions of people across Africa remain victims of protracted crisis, limited resources, social exclusion and broken infrastructure — locked out of the self-reliance that should be within reach. ARA was formed to treat that as a solvable, structural problem rather than an endless emergency.

—

What "Facilitating Development" means

Our Facilitating Development initiative finds solutions to challenges without a pre-established agenda, and builds the ability to recruit local or locally experienced volunteers and staff who already understand the community they're working in.

—

Where we focus

Our emphasis sits across the Health, Education and Welfare spheres — the three areas where structural exclusion shows up most directly in people's daily lives.

"Institutions that might enable people to act decisively in their own long-term interests are either missing or distorted. Our work is to help rebuild them."
— ARA mission statement
