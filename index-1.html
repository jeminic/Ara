Where we work

Four fronts, one root cause.

Millions across Africa remain locked out of self-reliance by protracted crisis, broken infrastructure and social exclusion. We work each of these fronts at once, because none of them heal in isolation.

01 — Protection

Refugee & IDP support

Direct, practical assistance for refugees and internally displaced persons across Nigeria and the region, from first contact through to durable solutions.

Field response
02 — Health

Health & disease response

Community health initiatives addressing HIV/AIDS and other communicable disease in crisis-affected and displaced populations.

Gender-based violence response

04 — Welfare

Education & institutions

How we work

No pre-established agenda. Just the evidence, and the people closest to it.

01

Start from root causes

We study the structural and direct violence that produces displacement in the first place, rather than treating each crisis as an isolated event.

02

Recruit locally, always

Our field teams are volunteers and staff drawn from the communities we work in — people who already carry the local knowledge our programmes depend on.

03

Build institutions, not dependency

Durable change comes from stronger local institutions and informal structures, not from standing in for them indefinitely.

04

Go in without a fixed script

Every context sets its own agenda. We arrive to find solutions with communities, not to deliver a pre-packaged one.
